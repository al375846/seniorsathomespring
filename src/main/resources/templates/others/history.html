Our History


  • 2000 : Mr. and Mrs Williams got married and formed a small company in Spain to help the elderly to carry their day to day more easily.
  • 2005 : The company was renamed as Sah and it had the logo that it currently has.
  • 2010 : The company became known throughout Europe, so they built a new franchise in London and started the website.
  • 2015 : Messrs. Williams passed away and the company was inherited by their daughter Lara Williams.
  • 2020 : Nowadays it is one of the most famous companies since it is known worldwide.
