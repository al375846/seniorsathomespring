Services List

Seniors At Home is a project that aims to help older people so that they can be at home with the best possible comforts. For this we have contacted companies from all over the country so that anyone who needs it can have the help they deserve.
These are the services that we currently offer, but we will add other services as they are requested. So if there is any other service that you would like us to offer, please contact us and we will do our best meet your needs.



CATERING

We have contracts with some of the best catering companies in the country in order to provide great service to our clients. We offer a wide range of diets that adapt to the needs of each client, be it medical (such as a low-calorie, hypoglycemic or gluten-free diet), or for religious or moral reasons (such as vegetarian, vegan...) We have a home delivery service to facilitate the service and make it as comfortable as possible and without complications. Our delivery people are trustworthy people who have experience and will help you in any way possible and we have secure payment methods to prevent possible problems.




CLEANING

Our cleaning service guarantees a clean home without complications Thanks to the companies with which we have contracts, we can offer a flexible schedule that adapts to the needs of each client (whether it is during morning, afternoon, night, on weekdays or on weekends). Our cleaning staff are qualified, experienced and trustworthy people, so don't worry about leaving your home in our hands. We offer the best prices and provide the necessary materials, with various alternatives in case of any allergies. So do not hesitate to hire our services.




NURSING

The companies with which we have contracts have qualified personnel in all areas and, in this way, be able to offer the best nursing service at home, and adapt to the needs of each client. We also provide the necessary materials, so you don't have to worry about having the necessary tools. We also offer flexible hours to better adapt to each client, and all of this at the best price. If you have any questions about the service or if we cover certain needs, do not hesitate to contact us.
